Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

Who Looks Older? ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is an AI-powered entertainment app that estimates the apparent age of two people from a live camera feed and declares which one "looks older." It is designed purely for fun and is not intended to provide accurate age assessments.

This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information. We are committed to transparency and to protecting your privacy.

1. Camera & Face Data

All face processing happens entirely on your device. No face images, facial geometry, or biometric data are ever saved to disk, uploaded to a server, or transmitted over any network.

The App uses your device camera to:

  • Detect faces in real time using Google ML Kit (on-device).
  • Estimate apparent age using a TensorFlow Lite model (on-device).

Camera frames are processed in memory in real time and immediately discarded. The only output retained is the estimated age as a simple integer, which is held temporarily in memory for displaying results and is not persisted after you close the App.

2. Biometric Data (BIPA / GDPR / State Laws)

The App processes facial geometry transiently in device memory solely to produce an age estimate. This data:

  • Is never written to persistent storage (disk, database, or file system).
  • Is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.
  • Is never used to identify any individual.
  • Exists only for the fraction of a second needed to run inference, then is freed from memory.

Because no biometric identifiers or biometric information are collected, stored, or shared, the App's processing does not constitute "collection" of biometric data under the Illinois Biometric Information Privacy Act (BIPA), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), or similar statutes.

3. Data We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. However, the following third-party services integrated into the App may collect certain information:

4. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob - We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data for ad serving and personalization. On iOS, ad personalization requires your consent via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt. If you decline tracking, AdMob will serve non-personalized ads only.

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses data from partner apps: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

RevenueCat - We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases (watermark removal and ad removal). RevenueCat may process purchase receipts and anonymized transaction data.

  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Who Looks Older? is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has used the App, please contact us at the address below and we will take steps to address your concern.

6. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS, the App will request your permission via Apple's ATT framework before allowing AdMob to access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA). You can change this setting at any time in your device's Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

7. Data Retention

The App does not persist any user data. Estimated age values are held in volatile memory only during a game session and are discarded when you leave the result screen or close the App. No data is written to local storage or remote servers by the App.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict its processing. Because the App does not collect or store personal data, there is generally no data for us to provide, correct, or delete.

For any data collected by third-party services (AdMob, RevenueCat), please refer to their respective privacy policies linked above, or contact us and we will assist where possible.

9. Security

Since all face processing occurs on-device and no personal data is transmitted or stored by the App, the primary security measure is the architecture itself: your data never leaves your device. We do not operate servers that receive or store user data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or data-related requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

fukuidinotech@gmail.com

12. Disclaimer

Who Looks Older? provides AI-powered age estimates for entertainment purposes only. Results are not accurate and should not be relied upon for any purpose. The App does not claim to determine actual age, and no decisions should be made based on its output.

プライバシーポリシー

発効日: 2026年3月28日

Who Looks Older?（以下「本アプリ」「当方」）はAIを活用したエンタメアプリで、カメラに映った2人の見た目の年齢を推定し、どちらが「年上に見えるか」を判定します。あくまで遊びを目的としており、正確な年齢判定を提供するものではありません。

このプライバシーポリシーは、本アプリがあなたの情報をどのように扱うかを説明します。当方は透明性とプライバシー保護に努めています。

1. カメラと顔データ

顔の処理はすべてあなたの端末内で完結します。 顔画像、顔の幾何データ、生体情報がディスクに保存されたり、サーバーにアップロードされたり、ネットワークを介して送信されることは一切ありません。

本アプリはカメラを以下の目的で使用します:

  • Google ML Kit（オンデバイス）でリアルタイムに顔を検出
  • TensorFlow Lite モデル（オンデバイス）で見た目の年齢を推定

カメラのフレームはメモリ内でリアルタイム処理され、即座に破棄されます。残るのは推定年齢の整数値のみで、結果表示のため一時的にメモリ内に保持され、アプリを閉じた後は保持されません。

2. 生体データ（BIPA / GDPR / 各州法）

本アプリは年齢推定を行うために、顔の幾何データを端末メモリ内で一時的に処理します。このデータは:

  • 永続的なストレージ（ディスク、データベース、ファイルシステム）に書き込まれることはありません。
  • サーバー、クラウドサービス、第三者に送信されることはありません。
  • 個人を特定する目的で使用されることはありません。
  • 推論の実行に必要なごく短時間のみ存在し、その後メモリから解放されます。

生体識別子や生体情報を収集・保存・共有しないため、本アプリの処理はイリノイ州生体情報プライバシー法（BIPA）、EU一般データ保護規則（GDPR）、カリフォルニア州消費者プライバシー法（CCPA）等における生体データの「収集」には該当しません。

3. 当方が収集するデータ

本アプリ自体は個人データを一切収集、保存、送信しません。ただし、本アプリに統合されている以下のサードパーティサービスが特定の情報を収集する場合があります。
